About

UX Engineer.
Digital Producer.
Still both.

I'm a UX Engineer and Digital Producer with 20 years of experience living at the intersection of design and code. My career started in print design, evolved into interactive and front-end development, and led to 11 years at WIHP/Cendyn building 250+ hotel and lifestyle websites — each one bespoke, each one a chance to push the craft further.

I don't just build what I'm handed. I think about why it should work the way it does, and how to make it easier to build next time. I've built Sass frameworks, component libraries, and multi-brand CSS architectures — not because it was asked for, but because it made everything downstream better.

I specialize in translating designs into pixel-perfect, accessible, responsive code. I spot friction in a production process and build the system that removes it.
